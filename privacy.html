Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Mavora ("the app", "we") is a personal finance and cheque-management app built to keep your data private. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

In short: Your financial records stay on your device. We have no account system, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking. The only time data leaves your device is when you choose to use the AI assistant.

1. Data stored on your device

All the records you create — cheques, transactions, invoices, clients, projects, goals, and settings — are stored locally on your device. We do not have a server that holds your records, and we cannot see them.

2. The AI assistant

When you ask the AI assistant a question, the app sends a computed summary of the relevant figures (for example totals and due dates) together with your question to our processing service, which forwards it to our AI provider (OpenAI) to generate a written answer. We do not send your raw account access, login details, or your full database. This data is used only to generate your answer in that moment; it is not used to build a profile of you, is not linked to your identity, and is not used to train AI models. If you never use the AI assistant, no financial data leaves your device.

3. Face ID / device authentication

If you enable app lock, authentication is handled entirely by Apple on your device. Your biometric data never reaches us and never leaves your device.

4. Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive your card or payment details. Apple provides us only with the subscription status needed to unlock features.

5. Backups

If you create a backup, the file is encrypted and saved wherever you choose (such as Files or iCloud). You control that file; we do not have access to it.

6. Data we do not collect

  • No accounts, names, or email sign-up inside the app
  • No advertising identifiers and no third-party ad networks
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking SDKs

7. Children

Mavora is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it. Our AI processing service does not retain your questions or figures after the answer is returned.

9. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new date.
