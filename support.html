How is my data stored?

Everything you enter stays on your device. There is no account and we never see your records. See our Privacy Policy.

How does the AI assistant work?

It sends only a computed summary of your figures plus your question to generate an answer. It never sends your full database, and the data isn't used to train AI models or build a profile.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Mavora. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel there at any time.

I paid but features are locked — how do I restore?

Open Mavora → Settings/Profile → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — paid plans include a 5-day free trial. You can cancel before it ends to avoid being charged.

How do I turn on Face ID lock?

Open Settings inside the app and enable app lock. Authentication happens entirely on your device.
